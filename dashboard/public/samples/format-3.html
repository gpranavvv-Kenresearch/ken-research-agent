The aerospace industry's problem is not orders, it is building fast enough to clear a backlog stretching past 2030. The global aerospace manufacturing market is valued at USD 913 billion in 2024 and is projected to approach USD 1.08 trillion by 2028 at a 4.2% CAGR, a climb the Global Aerospace Manufacturing Market Report maps across product, component and region. Commercial aircraft already drive 43.5% of output and 70% of growth, with narrow-body jets alone making up 60% of the commercial fleet as airlines chase efficiency against fuel costs running 42% of operating expense.
